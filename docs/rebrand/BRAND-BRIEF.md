# What An Adventure Games — Brand Direction Brief

## Studio Philosophy
"We're all about 'the journey' at What An Adventure Games. Journey before destination. That's what adventure is all about — where you are right now, the ups and downs and people you spend your time with, that's what it's all about. The journey is what shapes you and helps you become who you are and hopefully who you were meant to be. Sometimes we get back from a long journey or look back at something we really can't fathom experiencing at the moment and say 'What an Adventure!' Because that adventure changed you. You are more than you once were, through the good times and the bad, you're still adventuring. So adventure on and have your kids and parents join in on the fun and set off on some of our mini adventures that, maybe like the 'road less traveled by'… Make all the difference…"

## Audience
Gaming studio for kids and adults. Retro, modern and 3D — we're trying it all. Right now it's all an adventure!

## Visual Direction
- **LIGHT THEMED** — bright, airy, adventurous
- White/cream backgrounds, medium contrast text
- Accent colors from the Adventure Logo
- Feel: wonder, discovery, warmth, excitement
- NOT dark, NOT corporate, NOT minimal-zen — it's playful and energetic
